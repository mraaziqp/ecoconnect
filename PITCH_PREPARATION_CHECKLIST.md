## 🎯 TALKING POINTS (Memorize These)

### When they ask "How is this different from X?"
> "Nexus Hub is purpose-built for family wealth management. It's not a generic platform adapted for wealth - it's designed specifically for families, advisors, and the complexities of multi-generational wealth. The Estate Vault, Family Milestones, and Advisor Dashboard are features you won't find elsewhere."

### When they ask "Is it secure?"
> "Absolutely. All data is encrypted both at rest and in transit. We're SOC 2 compliant and POPI-ready (important for South African regulations). We maintain an audit trail of every action for compliance purposes. Your data never leaves South Africa."

### When they ask "How long to implement?"
> "Pilot is 3 months: Month 1 is setup and migration, Month 2 is training and feedback, Month 3 is optimization. Your team can be live with 5 clients in 90 days. Then scaling to your full book is straightforward."

### When they ask "What about integration?"
> "We have REST APIs that connect to most accounting systems (Xero, FNB, etc.). We're also building South African banking integrations. If you need something custom, we can build it as a service engagement."

### When they ask "What does it cost?"
> "Setup is R75,000 for the pilot. Then we charge per family office per month - typically R3,000-R5,000 depending on complexity. You can also charge your clients for the access, so it's net-positive. The ROI comes from your team's time savings and new recurring revenue."
